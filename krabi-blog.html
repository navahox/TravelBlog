Krabi: Nature's Masterpiece

Discover the Natural Wonders of Krabi

1. Railay Beach

Railay Beach is a stunning peninsula accessible only by boat, known for its towering limestone cliffs, clear waters, and white sandy beaches. It's a paradise for rock climbers and beach lovers alike, offering breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere.

Entrance Fee: Free

Dress Code: Beachwear

How to get there: Accessible by boat from Ao Nang or Krabi Town.

View on Google Maps

2. Emerald Pool

Located in the Thung Teao Forest Natural Park, the Emerald Pool is a natural hot spring surrounded by lush rainforest. The crystal-clear water and serene environment make it a perfect spot for a refreshing swim and a nature walk.

Entrance Fee: 200 THB

Dress Code: Swimwear

How to get there: Located about 70 km from Krabi Town, accessible by car or tour.

View on Google Maps

3. Phi Phi Islands

The Phi Phi Islands are a group of stunning islands famous for their turquoise waters, vibrant marine life, and dramatic cliffs. They offer excellent opportunities for snorkeling, diving, and island hopping, making them a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

Entrance Fee: 400 THB (Marine Park Fee)

Dress Code: Casual/Swimwear

How to get there: Accessible by ferry or speedboat from Krabi or Phuket.

View on Google Maps
