Pattaya: Nightlife and Beyond

Explore Pattaya Day and Night

1. Pattaya Beach

During the day, Pattaya Beach is a hub for water sports enthusiasts and sunbathers. Visitors can enjoy activities such as jet skiing, parasailing, and relaxing by the sea. The beach is lined with cafes and restaurants where you can savor local and international cuisine.

Entrance Fee: Free

Dress Code: Beachwear

How to get there: Pattaya Beach is centrally located and easily accessible by foot from most hotels in the area.

View on Google Maps

2. Walking Street

As night falls, Walking Street transforms into the epicenter of Pattaya's nightlife. With its vibrant mix of bars, clubs, and live music venues, it's a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the city's energetic nightlife. The street is bustling with neon lights and an exciting atmosphere.

Entrance Fee: Free

Dress Code: Casual

How to get there: Walking Street is located at the southern end of Pattaya Beach Road and is easily accessible by foot or local transportation.

View on Google Maps

3. Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden

Nong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden offers a serene escape with its beautifully landscaped gardens and cultural shows. Visitors can explore themed gardens, enjoy traditional Thai performances, and even see elephants. It's an ideal spot for a family outing or a peaceful day trip.

Entrance Fee: 500 THB

Dress Code: Casual

How to get there: Nong Nooch Garden is located about 30 minutes south of Pattaya. It is accessible by taxi or private car.
