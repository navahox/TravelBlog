Hua Hin: Royal Retreat

Discover the Charm of Hua Hin

1. Hua Hin Beach

Hua Hin Beach is a long stretch of pristine sand offering a peaceful retreat with gentle waves, perfect for swimming and sunbathing. It's a favorite getaway for both locals and tourists.

Entrance Fee: Free

Dress Code: Beachwear

How to get there: Hua Hin is approximately 200 km south of Bangkok. You can reach Hua Hin by car, which takes about 3 hours via Highway 35 and Highway 4. Alternatively, you can take a train from Hua Lamphong Station or a bus from the Southern Bus Terminal in Bangkok.

View on Google Maps

2. Mrigadayavan Palace

Known as the "Palace of Love and Hope," this beautiful teak palace is set amidst lush gardens and offers a glimpse into the royal past. It's a must-visit for history enthusiasts and architecture lovers.

Entrance Fee: 30 THB

Dress Code: Respectful attire required

How to get there: The palace is located between Cha-Am and Hua Hin, accessible by car or taxi from Hua Hin town center.

View on Google Maps

3. Hua Hin Night Market

The Hua Hin Night Market is a bustling hub of activity, offering a variety of local foods, souvenirs, and crafts. It's the perfect place to experience the local culture and enjoy delicious street food.

Entrance Fee: Free

Dress Code: Casual

How to get there: Located in the heart of Hua Hin, the night market is easily accessible by foot from most central hotels or by tuk-tuk.
